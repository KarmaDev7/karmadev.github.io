About MBF Group

MBF Group Holdings & Establishments is a progressive and diversified group based out of dubai, UAE with interests in Healthcare, IT and Retail spanning across the MENA region
Founder, HH Sheikh Mohammed Bin Faisal Al Qassimi is an alumnus of American University of Sharjah with extensive experience in Financial Markets, investment banking and deep networks across the region. Passionate about actively contributing to socio-economic and all round development, HH Sheikh Mohammed has also been one of the Founding Members of the Alumni Association of American University of Sharjah.
